# STITCH Prompt — Terrastone

## Product Context
Terrastone is a SaaS platform designed for indie game developers and small studios specializing in fantasy and strategy genres. It provides robust tools for organizing and managing game assets, fostering community engagement, and streamlining development workflows, allowing creators to focus on bringing their epic worlds to life.

## Design Style
Style: Dark Fantasy Strategy Game, Mystical, Robust
Mood: Powerful, immersive, trustworthy, epic, efficient
Inspiration: kingdomofarcane.com

Text: #e8e8f0
Accent: #ffb800

## Typography

Body font: Inter, 400
